Our Story

Why Al-Aameer Holidays

Founded in Kunzer, Baramulla in 2008, Al-Aameer Holidays was born from a single belief: that this valley deserves to be shown with care and honesty. Our founders — locally born travel enthusiasts from Baramulla — crafted the agency around slow, immersive journeys rather than rushed package tours.

Every guide is a local. Every houseboat captain knows Dal Lake like the lines of their palms. Every route is chosen for what you'll feel, not just what you'll see.
